## Example 1 — LinkedIn Post

**BEFORE (AI):**
> In today's ever-evolving digital landscape, it is paramount to leverage cutting-edge technologies to remain competitive. The multifaceted nature of artificial intelligence presents a testament to human ingenuity. As we navigate these transformative advancements, it is important to note that organizations must harness AI's full potential. In conclusion, embracing innovation is not just a choice — it's a necessity.

Issues: 9 kill-list words, 3 em dashes, hollow closer, filler opener, zero concrete detail, zero personal voice.

**DRAFT REWRITE:**
> AI is reshaping what 'competitive' means, faster than most companies are prepared for. What took a team of analysts a week three years ago now takes 20 minutes with the right tools. I've watched companies hesitate on this and lose ground they never got back. The ones moving ahead aren't necessarily the biggest. They're the ones willing to actually use the technology, not just talk about it.

**Self-audit — remaining AI tells:**
- "reshaping" is close to "transformative" territory — slightly corporate
- "prepared for" is fine but passive; could sharpen

**FINAL REWRITE:**
> AI is changing what 'competitive' means — fast. What took a team of analysts a week three years ago now takes 20 minutes with the right tools. I've watched companies hesitate on this and lose ground they never got back. The ones moving ahead aren't necessarily the biggest. They're the ones willing to actually use the technology, not just talk about it.

Changes: Specific timeframe added, personal observation, contractions, varied sentence lengths (11/25/19/10 words), one intentional em dash for emphasis, no kill-list words.

## Example 2 — Academic Paragraph

**BEFORE (AI):**
> The intricate tapestry of historical events that led to World War I is multifaceted and requires meticulous examination. Pivotal factors include nationalism, imperialism, and the complex interplay of alliances. It is important to note that the assassination of Archduke Franz Ferdinand served as a catalyst, bolstering pre-existing tensions. In essence, the war was not the result of a single event but rather a culmination of decades of political instability.

Issues: tapestry, multifaceted, meticulous, pivotal, served as (copula avoidance), bolstering, in essence, rule of three, meta-filler.

**DRAFT REWRITE:**
> World War I didn't start with a gunshot. The assassination of Archduke Franz Ferdinand on June 28, 1914, was the spark — but the powder had been building for decades. Nationalism had made European states increasingly aggressive toward each other. Imperial rivalries over Africa and Asia created grievances that no diplomat managed to resolve. And a web of alliances meant that when Austria-Hungary moved against Serbia, every major power got dragged in within weeks.

**Self-audit — remaining AI tells:**
- One em dash in a short passage — acceptable but note it
- "increasingly aggressive" is slightly formal — could stay for academic register
- Last sentence is long and list-like — break it up

**FINAL REWRITE:**
> World War I didn't start with a gunshot. The assassination of Archduke Franz Ferdinand on June 28, 1914, was the spark, but the powder had been building for decades. Nationalism had made European states aggressive in ways that kept escalating. Imperial rivalries over Africa and Asia created grievances no diplomat managed to resolve. When Austria-Hungary moved against Serbia, every major power got dragged in within weeks. The war was almost overdetermined by 1914 — the real question is why it didn't happen sooner.

Changes: Specific date, fragment opener, broken up final sentence, analytical voice in closer, no kill-list words.

## Example 3 — Blog Post

**BEFORE (AI):**
> In the realm of personal finance, it is crucial to utilize comprehensive strategies to achieve financial freedom. Leveraging diverse investment portfolios is a multifaceted approach that can bolster your financial stability. Furthermore, meticulous budgeting serves as a foundational pillar for long-term wealth accumulation. It is worth noting that commencing your investment journey early is paramount to maximizing compound interest.

Issues: realm, utilize, comprehensive, leveraging, multifaceted, bolster, furthermore, meticulous, serves as (copula), foundational pillar, commencing, paramount.

**DRAFT REWRITE:**
> Most people overthink personal finance. The basics work: spend less than you earn, invest the difference, and start early. That's it. The compound interest math is brutal in the best way — £10,000 invested at 22 earns roughly 10x more by retirement than the same amount invested at 32. Budgeting isn't glamorous, but it's where most people's plans fall apart.

**Self-audit — remaining AI tells:**
- "The basics work: [list of three]" — rule of three, but here it's actually a valid list, not forced. Keep.
- Reads clean. No obvious AI tells. The em dash is used once, purposefully.

**FINAL REWRITE:**
> Most people overthink personal finance. The basics genuinely work: spend less than you earn, invest the difference consistently, and start as early as you can. That's it. The compound interest math is brutal in the best way — £10,000 invested at 22 earns you roughly 10x more by retirement than the same amount invested at 32. Budgeting isn't glamorous, but it's where most people's plans either hold up or fall apart.

Changes: Conversational opener, concrete math example, "That's it." as a single-sentence paragraph punch, no kill-list words, contractions throughout.

## Rhythm Pattern Quick Reference

**Goal — irregular, natural human rhythm:**
Long sentence that builds context and adds detail (22 words). Short punch (5 words). Medium sentence with a turn (14 words). Fragment. Question?

**Avoid — uniform AI rhythm:**
This sentence is about the same length. This sentence is about the same length. This sentence is about the same length. This sentence is about the same length.

**Paragraph length variety:**
- One-sentence paragraph. Just this.

- Three-sentence paragraph. Builds a point. Lands it.

- Five-sentence paragraph. Sets up context. Develops a point. Adds nuance. Closes with something that doesn't sound like a conclusion but functions as one.
